Makati Highschool Troupe

The opening and shutting of the power up are separated. The event began on Saturday with the introduction of the candidates for Mr. and Mrs. Makati Highschool 2023.
They also lit the flame of sportsmanship for each game. The closing ceremony concluded with the awarding of the Ms. and Mr. Makati Highschool as well as the winners of each game and dance at each grade level.

Power up is a component of the quarterly performance in which all of the Makati High School students and the Mapeh teachers collaborate as a single team, with each grade level having its own competition based on a prior Mapeh topic. In the power up sports and eSports event, we also witnessed a variety of competitions and tournaments.
